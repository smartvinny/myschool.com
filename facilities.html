Post Modern library

The Campus has a well equipped Library with a student capacity of over 10,000 students that serves both students and staff. It is designed to provide a high level of support to both the teaching and learning programs. In addition to the book collection. Students have access to a wide array of electronic.Multimedia materials and global information through the internet. Qualified librarians supported, ensure that students are given the level of guidance and support they require, hence enabling them again valuable information from this resource centre..

Largest Play Ground

The Campus has a variety of recreational facilities which include one swimming pool, one football field, a basketball pitch and table tennis facilities. The football field is also used for hockey and rugby events. There is an excellent team at the campus that ensures proper administering of sport activities at the campus

Times: Games are to be held on Friday afternoon, from 1 p.m on wards.

The computer lab
